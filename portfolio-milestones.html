Milestone Sessions

Every Stage. Every Smile.

Babies grow at an astonishing pace. Our milestone sessions are designed to capture the major chapters of baby's first year — sitter sessions around 6–9 months, cake smash sessions at one year, and everything in between. Sessions last approximately 60–90 minutes in our bright, comfortable studio. We provide a variety of setups and props to match your vision.
