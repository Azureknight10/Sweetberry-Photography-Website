My Story

A Love for Babies That Started Long Ago

My love for babies goes back as far as I can remember. From the time I was a little girl, I was always the first to hold a new baby at family gatherings, mesmerized by those tiny hands and the quiet wonder that a new life brings. That love never left me — it simply grew into a calling.

Over 20 years ago, I picked up a camera and discovered that photography was the perfect way to combine my love for families with my desire to preserve the moments that pass so quickly. I've since dedicated my career to photographing maternity, newborn, and family sessions — and every single session still fills my heart with the same joy as the first.

Based in Avon Park, Florida, Sweet Berry Photography has been honored as a Best of Florida Winner for 2023, 2024, and 2025 in the Maternity & Newborn category — a recognition that means the world to me because it comes from the families I serve.

I am fully trained in newborn safety posing and stay current on the latest industry best practices to ensure that every baby in my studio is handled with the utmost care. When you arrive for your session, my goal is simple: I want you to feel completely at ease, so that what shows in your images is pure, authentic emotion.
