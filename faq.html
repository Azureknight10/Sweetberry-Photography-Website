Booking & Timing

The ideal time for maternity portraits is between 28–36 weeks of pregnancy, when your bump is beautifully rounded but you're still comfortable moving around. We recommend booking early in or before your third trimester, as sessions fill up quickly — especially April through September.
We recommend booking your newborn session during your second trimester so your spot is reserved. Newborns are ideally photographed within the first 5–14 days of life, when they are sleepiest and most flexible for posing. We block tentative dates during your pregnancy and confirm once baby arrives.
Life with a newborn is unpredictable, and we completely understand. We allow one complimentary reschedule with at least 48 hours' notice. For newborn sessions, if baby arrives early or late, we'll work with you to find the best available window.

Session Details

Maternity and family sessions typically run 60–90 minutes. Newborn sessions are unhurried and can last anywhere from 2–4 hours — we always follow baby's pace, taking breaks for feeding, soothing, and changing as needed. Please don't plan anything immediately after a newborn session.
For maternity sessions, we provide a stunning selection of flowing gowns, lace wraps, and wardrobe pieces in the studio — at no extra charge. For family sessions, we recommend neutral, coordinated tones (creams, whites, soft blues, dusty rose, earth tones) and avoiding busy patterns or logos. We'll send a complete styling guide after you book.

We typically photograph baby solo first, then include family members at the end of the session. We recommend siblings join toward the end so they don't have to sit and wait too long.

Images & Galleries

The number varies by session. Maternity sessions typically include 10+ fully edited images, newborn sessions 20+, and family sessions 15+. We focus on quality over quantity — every image in your gallery is carefully selected and edited to our signature standard.
Your fully edited gallery will be delivered via a private online gallery link within 2–3 weeks of your session. From there you can download digital files (if included in your package) and order prints, albums, and wall art directly through the gallery.
