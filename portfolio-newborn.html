Newborn Sessions

The Tiniest Moments, Forever

Newborn sessions are ideally booked during your third trimester and scheduled within the first 5–14 days after birth when babies are most sleepy and flexible for posing. Your safety-certified photographer will guide every pose using gentle, proven techniques honed over 20+ years. Sessions last approximately 2–4 hours (we work entirely at baby's pace), and we provide all wraps, outfits, and props.
